07 · True 3D Form

Volumetric column — body of revolution

Particles fill a solid vertical cylinder in 3D. Because every point sits at (r·cos θ, y, r·sin θ), yaw rotation keeps all points on the same cylinder surface — near-side projects larger, far-side smaller — so you see a genuine rotating solid, not a flat silhouette. Move the mouse to spin it.
